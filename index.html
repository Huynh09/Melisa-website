Luxurious Wedding & Event Venue in Ho Chi Minh City

About Melisa Center

Melisa Center is a luxurious wedding and events venue located in Tan Phu District, Ho Chi Minh City. Spread across nearly 13,000 m², it offers 9 grand banquet halls with no interior pillars, creating an elegant and spacious environment.

Restaurant Details

  • Address: 85 Thoại Ngọc Hầu, Hòa Thạnh, Tân Phú, Ho Chi Minh City
  • Capacity: Up to 3,500 guests in 9 banquet halls
  • Style: Neo‑classical European architecture with modern interiors
  • Menu & Services: Buffet with Asian and Western dishes, wedding and corporate event planning
  • Promotions: Event packages start from 3,490,000 VND, including special offers like free tables, discount, complimentary items
